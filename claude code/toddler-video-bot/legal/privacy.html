Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2025

Tiny Minds Learning ("we", "us", or "our") operates an automated educational content channel for toddlers and children. This page describes how we handle information when our application interacts with TikTok's API.

1. Information We Access

Our application requests the following TikTok permissions:

  • user.info.basic – to verify the authorized account (display name, avatar).
  • video.upload / video.publish – to post educational videos to the authorized TikTok account.

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data from TikTok users or viewers.

2. How We Use Information

Access tokens obtained through TikTok OAuth are stored locally on the operator's device only, solely to automate video uploads. They are never transmitted to any third-party server.

3. Children's Privacy

Our content is educational material designed for children ages 1–8. We do not collect any data from children or viewers of our content.

4. Data Retention

OAuth tokens are stored locally and can be deleted at any time by removing the tiktok_token.json file.
